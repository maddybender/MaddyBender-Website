Leadership & Involvement

Throughout my college experience, I have remained actively involved in a variety of organizations and events that have shaped both my professional and personal growth. I was a member of Women in Business and Kappa Kappa Gamma, studied abroad in Italy through the CIMBA program, and completed the LIFE public speaking training course. During my time abroad, I was also honored to be voted "Best Personality" by my peers. I participated in a Mental Health Awareness 5K, played intramural volleyball all four years, and took pride in staying engaged both socially and academically. One of my proudest accomplishments was winning a case study competition during my internship at Caterpillar, where my team presented our solution at a sales development conference. These diverse experiences reflect my passion for leadership, collaboration, and continuous improvement.
